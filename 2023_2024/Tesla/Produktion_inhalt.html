Worldwide Factories

  1. Tesla Gigafactory 1 (Nevada, USA):

    Also known as Gigafactory Nevada, it plays a crucial role in manufacturing batteries and energy storage technologies for Tesla vehicles.

  2. Tesla Fremont Factory (California, USA):

    The Fremont Factory is one of the primary production facilities for Tesla vehicles, including Model S, Model 3, Model X, and Model Y.

  3. Tesla Gigafactory Shanghai (China):

    This Gigafactory is a significant production facility for electric vehicles, particularly Model 3 and Model Y, targeting the Asian market.

  4. Tesla Gigafactory Berlin-Brandenburg (Germany):

    Under construction as of January 2022, this Gigafactory is planned to handle electric vehicle production for the European market.

  5. Tesla Gigafactory Texas (USA):

    Under construction as of January 2022, this Gigafactory is expected to handle electric vehicle production for the North American market.
