Basic Info

Super Mario Bros Wonder is a platformer sidescrolling game that comes from the Super Mario series, which is a series of video games from the Japanese game console manufacturer and game developer Nintendo. The first game in the series was released in 1985 with Super Mario Bros. for the Famicom, initially exclusively in Japan. Shigeru Miyamoto, who still works for the company, is the creator of the game series and the character Mario. To date, the series' games have sold over 400 million copies worldwide, making Super Mario one of the best-selling game series to date.
