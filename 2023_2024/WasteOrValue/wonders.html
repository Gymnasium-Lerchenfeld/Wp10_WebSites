Wonders

In New Super Mario Bros Wonder there are plenty of so called wonder effects. These occur by picking up a Wonder Flower which completely changes the course you are playing. Here is a list of all wonder effects. !Warning Spoilers ahead! Wonders that repeat themselves aren't listed.

    W1-Pipe-Rock Plateau

  1. You can find this Wonder in the first course of the game. Pipes are coming to life and start moving around like big worms. You can reach bigger heights which helps you collecting collectables.
  2. In the course "Piranha Plants on Parade" the level turns into an autoscroller and Piranha Plants start singing a Song. It’s a very enjoyable Wonder.
  3. The Wonder of the course "Scram Skedaddlers" comes with a huge rain of superstars which turn you constantly invincible. Watch out! If you don’t a collect another Star within 10 Seconds the effect will stop.
  4. Don’t get knocked over from the Bulrush appearing in „Bulrush Coming Through!“ As the name states a stampede of bulrushes gets triggered and they will take you on a special route. A 60 second timer is given for collecting the Wonder Seed.
  5. In "Here Come the Hoppos" the Wonder Flower triggers many hoppos to roll around the course which leads to a hard time staying in control of Mario. This wonder is very short that’s why the Time Limit of 60 seconds is more than generous.
  6. The Wonder of the course "Rolla Koopa Derby" turns out to be a very good and long effect. Giant slopes with Koopas on rollerskates appear. As a result you will have to collect Wonder Tokens to reveal the Wonder Seed at the top of the Tree.
  7. The first encounter with an actual rhythm level is in "Angry Spikes and Sinkin‘ Pipes" music starts playing and you have to avoid spike balls by listening to the beat.
  8. Silhouette Levels? Yes there is! In the level "Sproings in the Twilight Forest" The player, the enemies and the background suddenly gets much taller. You can change the whole atmosphere by crouching.
  9. The Castle in W1 "Pipe-Rock Plateau Palace" serves a pretty simple wonder. Throughout the Course Pipes starts falling. Try not to be squished.

    W2-Fluff-Puff Peaks

  1. By collecting the Wonder Flower in "Outmaway Valley" you trigger a massive snowball on which you have to ride on to get to the end of the course.
  2. In "Pokipede Pass" as a result of hitting the wonder flower snow start’s falling. When they hit the ground they create snow blocks.
  3. One of the more exciting wonders is hidden in "Condarts Away". Suddenly your character starts walking on the walls in the background which completely changes the game style.
  4. More transformation with "Up ˋnˋ Down with Puffy Lifts" you turn into a Puffy lift and you need to dodge Spikeballs to reach the Wonder seed.
  5. The course gets to a rhythm parcour in "Jump! Jump! Jump!"
  6. Free fall with Mario in "Countdown to Drop Down".
  7. By collecting a wonder flower in "Cruising with Linking Lifts" you trigger 3 Lakitus on cloud to spawn. They will throw items and wonder tokens at you.
  8. In the battleship "Fluff-Puff Peaks Flying Battleship" a giant bowser head with a cannon aims at the player trying to shoot them.

    W3-Shining Falls

  1. In "The Hoppycat Trial: Hop, Hop and Awaaay" giant Hoppycats will appear that can break grounds and other blocks.
  2. Hmm? Something is fishy in "The Anglefish Trial: Ready, Aim, Fly!". Multiple Anglefish start flying through the course which allows the player to reach new heights.
  3. "The Midway trial: Hop to It" is another transformation level. By collecting the Wonder Flower you turn into a Hoppycat.

    W4-Sunbaked Desert

  1. Ever found yourself in a rush? This is the case in "Armads on the Roll". The time speeds up.
  2. In "The Desert Mystery" you’ve got to search an ancient temple to find the wonder seed.
  3. Like the name "Rolling-Ball Hall" states you have to avoid spike balls caused to go after you because the screen tilts.
  4. The "Ninji Jump Party" is another rhythm based level where you have to jump to the beat.
  5. In "Bloomps of the Desert Skies" the player becomes a balloon and with this upgrade you can reach everywhere you can fit through.
  6. How calm. By triggering the Wonder Flower in "Valley Fulla Snootles" the time will slow down.
  7. The Wonder Flower in "Secrets of Shova Mansion" causes a shova to push a giant Bowser statue. Get out of there before it crushes you.
  8. "Flight of the Bloomps" is one of the very exciting Wonder levels. A giant Wonder Bloomp appears which you have to ride on to get through the course.
  9. In the "Sunbaked Desert Palace" the wonder flower causes the course to speed up and slow down in repetition.

    W5-Fungi Mines

  1. In "Upshroom Downshroom" you encounter Halloween? The Wonder Flower triggers a huge party where moving pumpkins appear.
  2. Quiztime is followed by the level "Taily’s Toxic Pond" you have to get 3 questions right to get through it.
  3. Another Wonder Effect liked by the fandom is King Boos opera in the course "Light-Switch Mansion". You have to navigate between dangerous, singing doors and boos to reach the end.
  4. Transform into a Wubba in "An Uncharted Area: Wubba Ruins".
  5. The wonder effect from the course "Another Uncharted Area: Swaying Ruins" turns the surface to goo. You have to stay alive while you are sliding in several directions.

    W6-Deep Magma Bog

  1. More Transformation? In "Where the Rrrumbas Rule" the player transforms into a spike ball moving through the level like a bowling ball.
  2. By picking up the Wonder Flower in "Pull, Turn, Burn" you gain invincibility against lava.
  3. In "Hot-Hot Hot" multiple flying water bubbles appear.
  4. Hitting the Wonder Flower in "Wavy Ride through the Magma Tube" leads to a sort of bossfight against a statue firing boulders.
  5. You ride on a Dragon in "Dragon Boneyard".

    Petal Isles

  1. You collect Wonder Tokens in "Leaping Smackerel" while a huge Smackerel is trying to eat you.
  2. In "Robbird Cove" air and water switch places.
  3. Fly high in "Blewbird Roost". Blewbirds shoot giant rainbow bubbles where you can bounce on.
  4. Fly through the sky and dodge Koopas and lighting in "Downpour Uproar".
  5. The course "Jewel-Block Cave" contains a ginormous Konk crushing anything what is in its way.
  6. In "Gnawsher Lair" thousands of gnawsher hunt you. POW Blocks can be used to stun them for a few seconds.
  7. Finally. The Goomba transformation awaits you in "Maw-Maw Mouthful".
  8. "Missile Meg Mayhem". Just a level with missiles…many missiles.
  9. In "High-Voltage Gauntlet" you turn into metal Mario where electricity is your friend.
  10. A more unique Wonder Effect is the one in "Evade the Seeker Bullet Bills". It will spawn a ship with a cannon which you can fire whenever you want.
  11. Another Rhythm level in "KnuckleFest Bowser‘s Blazing Beats". Really it’s just a rhythm level.
  12. And finally in "The Final Battle! Bowser‘s Rage Stage" there won’t be a Wonder Flower. The whole level consists of multiple Wonder Effects mixed together.
